Astrophotography

Long-exposure photos of stars. Most pictures were taken at a shutter speed of 15 minutes. No professional star tracker used, just point and wait! The streaks are the trails of the stars that is the result of the earth revolving. Since these photos are taken over a long period of time, think 5+ mins, they record every movement and position. I love this type of photography, because it captures multiple minutes into one single image.
